# 🚗 Vehicle Parking Management System (VPMS)

## 📌 Overview
The **Vehicle Parking Management System (VPMS)** is a web-based application designed to automate and manage parking operations efficiently. It replaces traditional manual parking systems with a digital solution that ensures accuracy, security, and ease of use.

The system allows administrators to manage parking slots, track vehicle entries and exits, and generate reports. Users can register, park their vehicles, and view their parking history.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Provide a user-friendly platform for admins and users  
- Automate vehicle entry and exit processes  
- Maintain a centralized parking database  
- Reduce manual errors and improve efficiency  
- Ensure secure data management
